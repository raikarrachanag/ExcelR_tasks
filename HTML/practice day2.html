Animals

Animals are multicellular, eukaryotic organisms in the biological kingdom Animalia. With few exceptions,
animals consume organic material, breathe oxygen, are able to move ,
can reproduce sexually, and grow from a hallow sphere of cells, the blastula, during embryonic development.
